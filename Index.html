Event Rules & Judgement Criteria

Common Rules:

  1. Every participant will be getting a unique registration number, which will be used to announce their identity to the judges. Name of the participant/ teacher(gurus)/ choreographer should not be announced and may lead to disqualification.

  2. For each event, participants should adhere to the specified time limit. Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification.
  3. Participants will be shown/sounded warning signals before the time limit and will need to stop the performance at the time limit bell for elocution and storytelling.
  4. Only the participant will be allowed on stage - any assistance needed by the participants for set up/ clean up will be provided by the organizing team.

  5. All events will be solo events to be performed by one individual, with the exception of Group dance.

  6. Any materials brought to the stage must be cleared and removed from the stage on completion of their performance and the event premises before 6 PM.

  7. Participants and their families will be responsible for any properties/costumes/instruments etc. used for the performance. EMA is not responsible for any loss of property.
